# Agent 2: Prospect Analyst & Qualifier

## Purpose
Qualify prospects against ICP using Agent 1's research data.

## 100-Point Qualification Framework

### Company Fit (40 points)
| Criteria | Points | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Employee count | 15 | 100-300: full, 50-99 or 301-1000: partial |
| Industry match | 10 | Top industries: full, adjacent: partial |
| Tech stack fit | 15 | Has target ERP/DW: full, partial stack: partial |

### Trigger Quality (20 points)
| Criteria | Points | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Leadership change | 8 | New C-suite/VP in finance: full |
| Funding/growth | 5 | Recent funding round: full |
| Migration/initiative | 4 | Active automation project: full |
| M&A activity | 3 | Recent acquisition: full |

### Pain Alignment (20 points)
| Criteria | Points | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Direct pain quotes | 12 | Verbatim from LinkedIn/interviews: full |
| Inferred pain signals | 8 | Job postings, tech stack gaps: partial |

### Individual Fit (20 points)
| Criteria | Points | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Persona quality | 10 | Champion persona with budget influence: full |
| Product engagement | 10 | Active user: full, trial: partial, none: 0 |

## Score Tiers
- **90-100 (HOT)**: Immediate outreach, personalized sequence
- **80-89 (WARM)**: Priority outreach, standard personalization
- **70-79 (QUALIFIED)**: Standard cadence, use case-focused
- **60-69 (MARGINAL)**: Nurture campaign, low priority
- **<60 (DEPRIORITIZE)**: Archive, revisit quarterly

## Additional Outputs
- **Use case assignment**: Primary + secondary with confidence scores
- **Benefit pillar mapping**: Which core benefit to lead with
- **Competitive context**: What they're currently using vs. product value
- **Outreach strategy**: Cold pitch vs. expansion vs. upsell recommendation
- **Messaging validation**: Score against messaging guide (>80 required)

## Output
18+ new columns added to Sheet1 per prospect, including:
- Qualification score + breakdown
- Tier assignment
- Use case (primary + secondary)
- Benefit pillar
- Outreach strategy recommendation
- Messaging validation score
- Personalization hooks (3 per prospect)
